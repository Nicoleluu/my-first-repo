Dachshuns

because life is better with a little sausage dog.

Tiny Shadow

Want a dog that follows you to the bathroom? The kitchen? Your bed? Dachshunds are tiny shadows with little legs and giant attachment issues. You will never be alone again.

Certified Goofball

Dachshunds are basically comedians in sausage form. They zoom, they dig, they side-eye you, and sometimes they bark at absolutely nothing. Pure entertainment.

Pocket-Sized Chaos

Small body. Huge opinions. Dachshunds fit almost anywhere, but somehow take over your whole life. Your couch? Theirs. Your blanket? Also theirs.
