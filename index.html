About

I graduated from Rhode Island School of Design in Industrial Design and am currently studying Computational Design Practices at Columbia GSAPP. My work moves between products, systems, and environments, exploring how design shapes human behavior and space.

Why CDP

I joined CDP to expand my design practice into computation. Coming from industrial design, I became increasingly interested in how code, systems, and data can shape environments and interactions beyond individual objects.
