About Me

I graduated from RISD in Industrial Design and am currently studying Computational Design Practices at Columbia GSAPP. My background began in product design, but my interests gradually expanded into systems, interaction, and computational workflows.

Why CDP

I joined CDP because I wanted to expand my design practice into the computational field. Coming from industrial design, I became increasingly interested in how computation can shape systems, behaviors, and environments beyond the scale of individual objects.
