Dachshuns

why should we become a dachshund owner?

Loyal

Dachshunds are deeply loyal and attached to their humans. They will follow you around, sleep beside you, and always want to be near you.

Funny

Their long bodies and tiny legs already make them funny, but their stubborn and dramatic personalities make everyday life even better.

Perfect Size

Small enough for apartments, but big enough to fill your home with energy. They are the perfect balance between cute and adventurous.
